Instructions for Using ChenChat in your Browser:
  1. Click "Sign In" on the home page.
  2. Enter your Google username and password into the pop-up window.
  3. After you've finished signing in to Google, you will be redirected to the "Find Your Chat Group" page. Here, you can enter a new or existing group name and the Gmail addresses of anyone you would like to invite to this group. If inviting multiple people, make sure each address is separated by a comma. They will be sent an email asking them to join your group chat.
  4. After pressing submit, you will be redirected to your group chat where you can see all messages that were previously sent as well as any new messages. To send a message type in the box at the bottom. When you're done, either press enter or the "send" button.
  5. If you'd like change group chats or invite more people, click the "back" button in the upper left corner.
Instructions for Using ChenChat with Google Home:
  1. Say either "hey Google" or "ok Google" to Google Home
  2. Immediately after, say "Talk to C Chat." This will start ChenChat on your Google Home
  3. Once the application is started, you can give the application a variety of commands.
    • Send for help
      • examples: "Help me," "Call for help!," "Send help"
    • Say how you're feeling
      • examples: "I want to go to bed," "I'm thirsty!," "Starving!"
  4. Close the application by saying "leave chat," "I'm done chatting", or "close app."
